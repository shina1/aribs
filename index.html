Who we are

About Our Company

We are professional event managers skilled in the technical as well as the creative and logistical implementation of events. Whether it's a Conference,Birthday party,Product launch,Wedding,cocktail party,awards ceremony or a get together. We go out of our way regardless of your budget to provide a rich and satisfactory experience for you.

Services

Event Coordination

We specialize in developing innovative ways to inspire,communicate and entertain. We take care of the most important aspect of event production including lighting, decoration, theming,styling,catering,sound and entertainment.

Conference and venue sourcing services.

Let's work with you to create a unique conference identity with careful programming of content and detailed logistic planning that include:--

      Ground transportation
      Delegate accomodation booking and venue sourcing
      Social and partner activities
      Engagement of guest speakers and facilitators
      Management of all audiovisual needs and much more

Special event planning services

We are here to go through the event planning stress for you by providing beautiful centerpieces, comfortable environment,stunnig decorations and floral arrangements, and funky entertainments options to make your special event perfect.
